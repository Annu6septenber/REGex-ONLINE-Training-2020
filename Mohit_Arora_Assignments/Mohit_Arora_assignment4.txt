Q2. String "hello this side regex", Find out the count of that vowels.
Ans:	a="hello this side regex"
	b=['a','e','i','o','u']
	count=0
	for x in a:
		for y in range(0,len(b)):
			if x in b[y]:
				count+=1
	print(count)
